# Brand Facts Pack

Consolidated brand hard-data handoff for Level 4. This pack exists so Insight
Strategy does not discover missing brand facts only at the decision stage. It
unifies what the input package currently scatters across `Brand Context`,
`Brand Facts`, and `Strategy Readiness Pack`.

Fill what exists. For every empty or weak field, set a confirmation status so
Level 4 can decide between a Final and a Provisional recommendation. Do not
invent brand truth to fill a blank — an honest gap is more useful than a
fabricated fact.

Per-field confidence: `confirmed` (brand-owned/official source), `reported`
(third-party or user-reported), `inferred` (analyst hypothesis), or `missing`.
