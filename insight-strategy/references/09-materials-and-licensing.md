# Materials And Licensing

Use this file when adding templates, lexicons, or external reference material to
the skill.

## Built-In By Default

These can live inside the skill because they are project-specific structures:

- Input package template
- Evidence pool and evidence matrix templates
- Human truth record
- Cultural tension record
- Idea Platform record
- Concept record
- Starter topic-tag taxonomy
- Small starter marker list used by the optional raw-evidence preparation script

## User-Supplied Or Licensed Separately

Do not bundle full third-party lexicons, agency templates, or proprietary
method documents unless the user provides a copy they are allowed to use.

Treat these as optional external materials:

- Full Chinese emotion or sentiment lexicon
- Full English emotion lexicon
- Internal Idea Platform, strategy, or concept-record templates
- Historical campaign decks and case studies
- Brand books, product proof libraries, or proprietary research

## How To Use Missing Materials

If a useful material is missing, keep the workflow moving with a clearly marked
starter structure. Mark outputs as provisional only when the missing material
affects the strategic decision, not merely because a preferred template is
unavailable.
