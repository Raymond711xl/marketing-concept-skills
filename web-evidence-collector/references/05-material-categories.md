# Material Categories

Use this file to collect category-specific evidence without doing analysis.

## Visual Materials

Collect posters, KVs, landing pages, packaging, product pages, social visuals, event visuals, thumbnails, screenshots, and campaign case images.

Fields to prioritize:

- Material type
- Source and URL
- Screenshot status
- Visual observation: composition, hierarchy, color, typography, imagery, CTA
- Campaign/message
- Platform/channel
- Related evidence
- Restriction note

## Video

Collect video ads, TVCs, campaign films, short videos, livestream clips, product films, and brand films.

Fields to prioritize:

- Video title or asset name
- Platform and URL
- Release date
- Brand/campaign
- Short quote or slogan if visible
- Observation: opening hook, narrative setup, product role, visual style
- Metric/count if visible: views, likes, comments, reposts, awards, media mentions
- Related evidence across PR, social, landing page, or offline activation

## Offline Activation

Collect pop-ups, exhibitions, retail events, installations, stunts, roadshows, campus/community events, and experiential activations.

Fields to prioritize:

- Event name
- Location and time
- Source and URL
- Participation mechanism
- Visual or spatial material
- Social sharing trigger
- Online amplification evidence
- Metric/count if visible: attendance, posts, media articles, wait time, visitor count
- Related evidence across video, PR, marketing, or social

## Marketing

Collect launches, collaborations, offers, ecommerce moments, membership actions, private traffic, KOL/KOC activity, UGC challenges, platform mechanics, and conversion paths.

Fields to prioritize:

- Mechanism
- Channel
- Offer/hook/incentive
- Partner/creator/platform
- Timing and campaign window
- Metric/count if visible: sales claims, coupon use, hashtag volume, engagement
- Related evidence across video, offline, PR, or visual materials

## PR

Collect press releases, media coverage, interviews, announcements, awards, controversy, crisis statements, public issue responses, and reputation events.

Fields to prioritize:

- News angle
- Speaker/institution
- Media/source spread
- Message frame
- Date and publication
- Short quote
- Public response signal if available
- Risk or controversy evidence
- Related evidence across video, offline, marketing, or social

## Social Leads

Collect public posts, hashtags, comments, platform search results, user screenshots, and user exports.

Fields to prioritize:

- Platform
- Account/author when public
- Date if visible
- One short key phrase
- Link
- Metric/count if visible
- Restriction note
- Whether the item needs manual user verification

## Reports / Market Context

Collect reports only when they support the user's research scope.

Fields to prioritize:

- Report title
- Publisher
- Date
- URL or file/page citation
- Short quote or key data point
- Summary
- Source level
- Limitations
