# Coverage And Statistics

Use this file to report collection completeness and skew.

## Why Counts Are Not Enough

Category counts alone do not prove balanced evidence. Also check:

- Source diversity
- Platform diversity
- Brand-owned vs third-party vs audience source mix
- Campaign/message repetition
- Whether the same concept appears across multiple channels
- Whether one campaign dominates the pool
- Whether social evidence is accessible or only represented by weak leads

## Skew And Completeness Check

Report skew with specific cause:

- Category skew: too much PR, not enough visual/video/offline.
- Source skew: mostly brand-owned, little third-party or audience evidence.
- Platform skew: one platform dominates social leads.
- Concept skew: one campaign/message dominates, limiting category read.
- Time skew: evidence concentrated in one launch week.
- Access skew: important platforms restricted or user-needed.

Use this format:

```markdown

| Check | Status | Evidence | Risk for downstream analysis | Suggested next collection |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |

```

## Metrics For Planning

When visible and sourced, preserve:

- View counts
- Likes/comments/reposts
- Article count by campaign/message
- Number of offline event reports
- Number of social leads by platform
- Number of materials under the same campaign concept
- Number of competitors using similar concepts

If metrics are not visible or volatile, mark `not available` or `volatile as of access date`.
